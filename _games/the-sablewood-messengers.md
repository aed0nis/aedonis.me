> “Marlowe Fairwind, the right-hand sorcerer of King Emeris, has gathered her most trusted allies to deliver a sealed package to Hush — a quiet village hidden deep within the ancient forest of Sablewood.
>The crate hums with magic, marked for the Whitefire Arcanist.
>The path is long, the forest alive, and whispers speak of hybrid beasts and forgotten gods…”


- **Level:** Pre-generated (choose from 5 heroes)
- **Length:** 3–4 hours
- **Players Needed:** 2–5
- **You’ll Need:** Two d12s of different colors ( Hope & Fear )

**What to Expect**
- Cinematic storytelling built on Hope & Fear
- Easy-to-learn rules – no prep needed
- Cooperative roleplay and meaningful choices
- A one-shot that can branch into a full campaign

**Who You’ll Play**
Choose from five pre-generated heroes (see players guide attached):
- Marlowe Fairwind – Loreborne Elf Sorcerer (royal mage, calm until she isn’t)
- Barnacle – Underborne Ribbet Rogue (a troublemaker who won’t swim)
- Garrick Reed – Highborne Human Warrior (a charming defector with a kind blade)
- Khari Nix – Ridgeborne Giant Guardian (loyal, unyielding, and born for battle)
- Varian Soto – Wildborne Katari Ranger (deadly hunter of hybrid beasts)

Each comes with unique backstory hooks and party connections to explore before play begins.
